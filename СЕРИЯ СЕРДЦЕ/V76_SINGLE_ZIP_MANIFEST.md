Core V76 formula:

```text
A recurring sin is not automatically a ruling sin.
A protected sin is never a small matter.
Weak grace may fight badly.
False grace negotiates peace.
The question is not whether sin is near the heart,
but whether the heart lets it sit on the throne.
```

Primary anchors:

- Boston, *Human Nature in its Fourfold State* — original sin as riverhead; predominant sin as strongest stream.
- Owen, *Indwelling Sin* / *Mortification* — remaining sin fights, grace has sovereign inclination, mortification is daily duty.
- Brooks, *Mute Christian* / *Precious Remedies* — proper/bosom/darling/master sin is hardest to subdue.
- Gurnall, *Christian in Complete Armour* — sincerity does not spare a darling lust.
- Baxter, *Christian Directory* / *Practical Works* — no known/wilful sin peacefully retained.
- Flavel, *Keeping the Heart* — discovering and mortifying bosom sin as practical godliness.
