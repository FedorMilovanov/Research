## Main contribution

V71 adds the pastoral layer needed after the heavy false-grace / temporary-faith passes:

```text
False peace can be bright but dead.
True grace can be dark but alive.
Melancholy can distort spiritual sight.
Desertion can remove comfort without removing union.
Satanic accusation can imitate conviction but drive away from Christ.
```
