# V72 — Backsliding / Apostasy / Decay / Repentance / Mortification

**Дата:** 2026-06-25  
**Режим:** продолжаем без распухания архива: один новый исследовательский том + точечные вставки в существующие файлы.  
**Тема:** как отличить падение/охлаждение настоящего верующего от апостасии, ложного покаяния, жизни в грехе и религиозного самообмана.

---

## 1. Зачем нужен V72

V68–V71 закрыли almost Christian, temporary faith, false peace, afflicted conscience, darkness/desertion/melancholy. Следующая пастырская дыра: **что делать с человеком, который не просто сомневается или страдает, а реально остывает, возвращается назад, падает, слабеет, начинает мириться с грехом?**

Нужна карта, которая не говорит легкомысленно: “раз ты упал, значит, не Христов”, но и не говорит дешёво: “раз когда-то был религиозный опыт, всё безопасно”.

Ключевая формула V72:

```text
A true believer may fall, decay, be wounded, and need restoration.
A temporary believer may flourish outwardly and then wither.
An apostate falls away from truth, holiness, or worship of the gospel.
Indwelling sin remains in believers as a law, but grace gives a prevailing will toward good.
Mortification is not self-repair: it is Spirit-work in those united to Christ.
Repentance is not legal terror, resolution, or selective reform; it is a grace that turns the soul from sin to God.
```

## 2. John Owen — apostasy is broader than “I had doubts”

**Primary source:** John Owen, *The Nature and Causes of Apostasy from the Gospel*.  
**Access:** CCEL HTML; Monergism PDF.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://www.ccel.org/ccel/owen/apostasy.i.v.html
- https://www.monergism.com/thethreshold/sdg/owen/The%20Nature%20and%20Causes%20of%20Apostasy%20-%20Owen.pdf

### Why this is gold

Owen begins with Hebrews 6:4–6 and announces that he is investigating the **nature, causes, and occasions of defection from the truth, holiness, and worship of the gospel**. This is a crucial map: apostasy is not simply “I feel spiritually weak” or “I am troubled by doubts.” It is a defection from the gospel in doctrine, holiness, or worship.

In later chapters Owen names causes such as spiritual darkness/ignorance, pride, vanity, sloth, negligence, love of the world, Satan’s work, and divine judgment. This makes apostasy concrete without turning every depression or fall into apostasy.

### Diagnostic value

| Pastoral question | Owen helps answer |
|---|---|
| Is apostasy only doctrinal denial? | No; Owen also treats defection from gospel holiness and worship. |
| Is every darkness apostasy? | No; darkness may be in the mind, but apostasy is defection from the gospel’s truth/holiness/worship. |
| Is worldliness “small”? | No; love of the world is one of the causes of defection. |
| Is apostasy sudden? | Often no; sloth, negligence, pride, and worldly love prepare the soul. |

### Article-use formulation

> Апостасия — это не всякая слабость и не всякая тьма. Но она редко начинается как громкий отказ от Христа. Часто она начинается как небрежность, духовная леность, гордость, любовь к миру, потеря вкуса к святости и тихое отступление от истины, святости и поклонения Евангелия.

## 3. John Owen — indwelling sin remains, but not as lord

**Primary source:** John Owen, *Nature, Power, Deceit, and Prevalency of the Remainders of Indwelling Sin in Believers*.  
**Access:** CCEL HTML; Monergism PDF; IA scan.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://ccel.org/ccel/owen/indwellingsin/indwellingsin.i.v.html
- https://www.monergism.com/thethreshold/sdg/owen/Indwelling%20Sin%20-%20John%20Owen.pdf
- https://archive.org/details/naturepowerdecei00owen

### Why this is gold

Owen’s opening distinction is article-critical: he treats indwelling sin **in persons after their conversion to God**. He says believers find a “law” of sin present with them; sin remains a powerful inward principle. But he also says grace keeps in believers a constant, ordinarily prevailing will toward spiritual good.

This gives an excellent diagnostic line:

```text
unbeliever: the will of sinning is not taken away;
believer: sin remains, but the will is bent toward good through grace.
```

### Diagnostic value

- A believer may know the power of indwelling sin more painfully than a careless unbeliever because the believer swims against the stream.
- The presence of conflict is not automatically proof of hypocrisy; it may be evidence that grace is alive.
- But a professing Christian who has no war, no grief, no will toward good, and no opposition to sin should not be comforted cheaply.

### Article-use formulation

> Живой человек чувствует болезнь иначе, чем мёртвый. Верующий узнаёт силу греха именно потому, что борется с ним. Но это не означает, что борьба сама по себе спасает; вопрос в том, чья воля господствует, куда направлен habitual bent сердца, и есть ли в душе реальное движение к добру ради Бога.

## 4. John Owen — mortification is Spirit-work, not self-repair

**Primary source:** John Owen, *Of the Mortification of Sin in Believers*.  
**Access:** CCEL HTML/PDF; IA edition.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://www.ccel.org/ccel/owen/mort.i.v.html
- https://ccel.org/ccel/owen/mort/mort.i.x.html
- https://www.ccel.org/ccel/o/owen/mort/cache/mort.pdf
- https://archive.org/details/ontemptationmort00owenuoft

### Why this is gold

Owen protects the series from two opposite errors:

1. **Cheap comfort:** “раз благодать, можно не убивать грех”. Owen insists that the best saint would fall into dreadful sins if he gave up mortification.
2. **Self-repair:** “я сам дисциплиной убью грех”. Owen insists that mortification is through the Spirit; without the Spirit, a man cannot truly mortify sin.

### Diagnostic value

```text
discipline without Christ = self-repair, not mortification;
comfort without mortification = presumption, not gospel;
mortification through the Spirit = grace fighting sin from union with Christ.
```

### Article-use formulation

> Умерщвление греха — это не попытка невозрождённой природы стать святой силой расписания. Но и благодать — не разрешение оставить грех жить. Настоящая благодать не только прощает вину, но и вооружает против господства греха.

## 5. Richard Sibbes — returning backslider, not instantly discarded sinner

**Primary source:** Richard Sibbes, *The Returning Backslider*, sermons on Hosea 14.  
**Access:** EEBO-TCP record; Digital Puritan PDF; Monergism eBook entry.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://quod.lib.umich.edu/e/eebo2/A12190.0001.001?view=toc
- https://www.digitalpuritan.net/Digital%20Puritan%20Resources/Sibbes%2C%20Richard/The%20Complete%20Works%20of%20Richard%20Sibbes%20%28vol.2%29%20Ind%20Works/%5BRS%5D%20The%20Returning%20Backslider.pdf
- https://www.monergism.com/returning-backslider-ebook

### Why this is gold

Sibbes’ title page alone is pastoral gold: Hosea 14 is opened to show the large extent of God’s free mercy even to miserable, forlorn sinners upon humiliation and repentance. This prevents a harsh diagnostic machine: there is such a thing as a backslider called to return, not merely a hypocrite to be discarded.

### Diagnostic value

| Error | Sibbes correction |
|---|---|
| “He fell; therefore there is no hope.” | Hosea 14 calls the backslider to return. |
| “Mercy means no humiliation or repentance.” | Mercy is offered upon humiliation and repentance. |
| “Restoration is cheap.” | Restoration comes by returning to the Lord, not managing appearances. |

### Article-use formulation

> Падение верующего — не повод объявить, что для него нет пути назад. Но возвращение — это не косметическое восстановление репутации; это возвращение к Господу с унижением, исповеданием и надеждой на свободную милость.

## 6. Richard Sibbes — danger of backsliding: the creature becomes a prop

**Primary source:** Richard Sibbes, *The Danger of Backsliding*.  
**Access:** Digital Puritan PDF.  
**Source URL:**

- https://www.digitalpuritan.net/Digital%20Puritan%20Resources/Sibbes%2C%20Richard/The%20Complete%20Works%20of%20Richard%20Sibbes%20%28vol.7%29%20Ind%20Works/%5BRS%5D%20The%20Danger%20of%20Backsliding.pdf

### Why this is gold

Sibbes says the heart naturally hangs loose from God and is soon ready to join with the creature; therefore the soul must meditate on Christ’s excellencies and adhere to Him. This fits the user’s current line on idols with precision: backsliding may begin not in open blasphemy but in the heart resting on creature-props.

### Diagnostic value

```text
lawful comfort becomes dangerous when it becomes a prop replacing Christ;
backsliding begins when the heart loosens from God and fastens to the creature;
meditation on Christ’s excellency is not decoration, but medicine.
```

### Article-use formulation

> Не всякое пользование творением — идолопоклонство. Но когда сердце начинает держаться за творение как за подпорку, убежище, радость и смысл вместо Христа, оно уже не просто “расслабилось”; оно скользит назад.

## 7. Thomas Brooks — Satan’s devices for growing, decaying, wandering souls

**Primary source:** Thomas Brooks, *Precious Remedies Against Satan’s Devices*.  
**Access:** EEBO-TCP; Digital Puritan PDF; Monergism PDF.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://quod.lib.umich.edu/e/eebo2/A77614.0001.001/1:5.12?rgn=div2;view=fulltext
- https://www.digitalpuritan.net/Digital%20Puritan%20Resources/Brooks%2C%20Thomas/Works%2C%20Vol.%20I/%5BTB%5D%20Precious%20Remedies.pdf
- https://www.monergism.com/thethreshold/sdg/brooks/PreciousRemediesAgainstSatanThomasBrooks.pdf

### Why this is gold

The 1653 title page says Brooks writes for people “in Christ, or out of Christ,” “growing” or “decaying,” “tempted” or “deserted,” having assurance or lacking assurance, wandering or staggering. This is a rare source that explicitly refuses one-size-fits-all diagnosis.

### Diagnostic value

Brooks is useful for a diagnostic chapter because he can structure Satan’s devices under several headings:

- devices to draw the soul to sin;
- devices to keep the soul from holy duties;
- devices to hinder holy services;
- devices to keep people off religious performances;
- devices by examples of the multitude;
- devices through discouragement, assurance issues, desertion, and bitterness.

### Article-use formulation

> Искушение не всегда выглядит как грубый соблазн. Иногда оно выглядит как “все так делают”, “сейчас не время молиться”, “раз нет чувства — не служи”, “раз ты упал — уже поздно”, “раз благодать — можно ослабить караул”. Пастырская диагностика должна распознавать не только грех, но и устройство ловушки.

## 8. Thomas Watson — counterfeit repentance vs true repentance

**Primary source:** Thomas Watson, *The Doctrine of Repentance* (1668).  
**Access:** Grace Gems HTML; IA 1668 microfilm entry; Monergism PDF.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://www.gracegems.org/Watson/repentance.htm
- https://www.gracegems.org/Watson/repentance2.htm
- https://archive.org/details/bim_early-english-books-1641-1700_the-doctrine-of-repentan_watson-thomas_1668/page/n3/mode/2up
- https://www.monergism.com/thethreshold/sdg/watson/The%20Doctrine%20of%20Repentance%20-%20Thomas%20Watson.pdf

### Why this is gold

Watson directly distinguishes counterfeit repentance from true repentance. In the modernized Monergism layout, the counterfeit section names legal terror, resolution against sin, and leaving many sinful ways alone. In the Grace Gems HTML, Watson defines repentance as a grace of God’s Spirit and gives the classic ingredients: sight of sin, sorrow for sin, confession, shame, hatred, turning from sin.

### Diagnostic value

| Counterfeit | Why it is not enough |
|---|---|
| Legal terror | Fear of punishment is not hatred of sin as sin. |
| Resolution | Promises under pressure may not be a new heart. |
| Leaving many sins | Selective reform may preserve a beloved lust. |
| Emotional sorrow | Tears may be about consequences, reputation, or fear. |

### Article-use formulation

> Истинное покаяние нельзя свести к страху, обещанию исправиться или отказу от нескольких грехов. Законный ужас может встряхнуть совесть, но благодать обращает сердце против самого греха и к Богу.

## 9. Jeremiah Burroughs — sin is worse than affliction

**Primary source:** Jeremiah Burroughs, *The Evil of Evils, or the Exceeding Sinfulness of Sin* (1654).  
**Access:** Monergism PDF; EEBO-TCP record; UPenn catalog entry.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://www.monergism.com/thethreshold/sdg/burroughs/The%20Evil%20of%20Evils%20-%20Jeremiah%20Burroughs.pdf
- https://quod.lib.umich.edu/e/eebo/A77976.0001.001?view=toc
- https://onlinebooks.library.upenn.edu/webbin/book/lookupid?key=ha008963720

### Why this is gold

Burroughs’ title-page thesis is excellent: there is more evil in the least sin than in the greatest affliction; sin is opposite to God, to man’s good, and to all good; sin is the poison of other evils. This is the medicine for the modern instinct: “главное, чтобы мне было не больно”.

### Diagnostic value

- A heart choosing sin to avoid pain is already making a terrible exchange.
- Affliction may be spiritually useful; sin never becomes good by good intentions.
- “Comfort” that requires sin is poison disguised as relief.

### Article-use formulation

> Один из признаков духовного упадка — когда человек начинает считать страдание худшим злом, а грех терпимой ценой за облегчение. Пуританская логика переворачивает это: лучше горькая скорбь под Богом, чем сладкое облегчение против Бога.

## 10. Thomas Watson — sin brings a person low

**Primary source:** Thomas Watson, *The Mischief of Sin* (1671).  
**Access:** EEBO-TCP record; Monergism PDF; Grace Gems HTML.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://quod.lib.umich.edu/e/eebo/A65303.0001.001?view=toc
- https://www.monergism.com/thethreshold/sdg/watson/The_Mischief_of_Sin_-_Thomas_Watson.pdf
- https://www.gracegems.org/Watson/mischief_of_sin1.htm

### Why this is gold

Watson’s controlling line is Psalm 106:43 — people are brought low for iniquity. He describes sin as something pleasant first and tragic afterward; his pastoral image is that a festering wound should be lanced rather than covered with silk.

### Diagnostic value

This is useful for warning without cruelty:

```text
do not cover a festering wound with religious silk;
do not call low spiritual condition “just circumstances” if iniquity is bringing the soul low;
do not lance a wound to shame the patient, but to heal.
```

### Article-use formulation

> Иногда человека опускает не темперамент, не занятость и не слабая дисциплина, а собственная беззаконие. Но даже тогда цель пастырского обличения — не публично унизить, а вскрыть гнойник, который нельзя исцелить шёлковой повязкой религиозных слов.

## 11. William Gurnall — spiritual warfare and relapse

**Primary source:** William Gurnall, *The Christian in Complete Armour*.  
**Access:** CCEL PDF; Digital Puritan text; IA PDF.  
**Source URLs:**

- https://www.ccel.org/ccel/gurnall/armour/files/gurnal01a.pdf
- https://www.digitalpuritan.net/Digital%20Puritan%20Resources/Gurnall%2C%20William/The%20Christian%20in%20Complete%20Armour.txt.html
- https://archive.org/download/christianincompl00gurnuoft/christianincompl00gurnuoft.pdf

### Why this is gold

Gurnall frames Christian life as war against the devil and says the Christian needs courage/resolution, must prosecute irreconcilable war against bosom sins, and must act faith on God’s almighty power for defense in temptations.

### Diagnostic value

Gurnall helps prevent soft moralism:

- relapse is not only “bad planning”; it is spiritual warfare;
- bosom sins require irreconcilable war, not negotiation;
- courage is not self-confidence but acting faith on God’s power;
- the saint must not confuse battle fatigue with surrender.

### Article-use formulation

> Духовная борьба — не театральная метафора. Если грех живёт у сердца как “домашний” грех, с ним нельзя заключать перемирие. Но победа не строится на мужестве плоти; вера должна пользоваться силой Божьей в самих стеснениях и искушениях.

## 12. Integrated diagnostic grid: fall, decay, backsliding, apostasy

| Condition | Key signs | Pastoral danger | Needed word |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ordinary remaining sin in believer | conflict, grief, will toward good, returning to Christ | calling conflict hypocrisy | gospel + mortification |
| Decay / cooling | negligence, less watchfulness, less relish for duties, creature-props | normalizing drift | awaken + restore means |
| Backsliding | real retreat, sin tolerated, duties neglected, heart loose from God | either despair or cheap reassurance | return to the Lord with repentance |
| Counterfeit repentance | terror, resolutions, selective reform, no hatred of sin | mistaking fear for grace | expose and call to Christ |
| Apostasy | defection from gospel truth, holiness, or worship | treating defection as mere weakness | solemn warning |
| Melancholy/desertion | darkness, fear, lack of assurance, but not love of sin | treating wounded sight as dead heart | tender comfort + careful examination |

## 13. Gold synthesis for the series

```text
Owen (Apostasy) = defection from gospel truth/holiness/worship.
Owen (Indwelling Sin) = sin remains in believers as a law, but grace gives a prevailing will toward good.
Owen (Mortification) = no cheap comfort; no self-repair; kill sin by the Spirit.
Sibbes (Returning Backslider) = there is a gospel road back for the humbled backslider.
Sibbes (Danger) = backsliding begins when the heart loosens from God and fastens to creature-props.
Brooks = Satan has devices for growing and decaying souls alike.
Watson (Repentance) = legal terror/resolution/selective reform are not true repentance.
Burroughs = sin is worse than affliction; do not choose sin to escape pain.
Watson (Mischief) = sin brings low; festering wounds need lancing, not silk.
Gurnall = relapse and resistance belong to real spiritual war.
```

## 14. Article-ready core paragraph

> Падение не всегда доказывает, что человек никогда не был жив; но и прошлый религиозный опыт не доказывает, что нынешнее отступление безопасно. Пуритане учили различать остаточный грех, духовный упадок, возвращаемое отступление, ложное покаяние и апостасию. Живой верующий может падать, темнеть, слабеть, но в нём остаётся воля к добру, скорбь о грехе, нужда во Христе и путь возвращения. Временный верующий может иметь религиозный расцвет, страхи, решения и реформу, но без корня. Апостасия же — это не просто слабость, а отступление от истины, святости и поклонения Евангелия. Поэтому сердце надо лечить не одним молотком, а точным словом: одних будить, других возвращать, третьих утешать, всех — вести ко Христу.
