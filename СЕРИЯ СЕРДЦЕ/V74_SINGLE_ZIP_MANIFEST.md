## Main V74 contribution

V74 adds a focused diagnostic layer on holy affections and counterfeit religious affections:

```text
No affection = no living religion.
High affection = no certain proof of saving grace.
True affection is rooted in a spiritual sight of Christ's excellency.
Counterfeit affection may be intense, verbal, joyful, zealous, and temporary.
Christ's loveliness is the positive object that draws and orders the renewed heart.
```

## Use rule

Use V74 to avoid both errors:

1. emotionalism: treating strong religious feeling as proof of grace;
2. dead formalism: treating all affection as suspicious and reducing Christianity to cold correctness.
