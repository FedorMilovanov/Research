Core V75 formula:

```text
A creature is not an idol because it is loved.
It becomes idolatrous when it receives the love, trust, fear, rest, hope, or obedience due to God.
Lawful comfort is gift under God.
Worldliness is gift above God.
```

Primary anchors:

- Baxter, *A Christian Directory*, lawful vs sinful love of creatures; end/order/predominance.
- Clarkson, *Soul Idolatry*, inward worship of creatures through trust/fear/dependence.
- Flavel, *Keeping the Heart*, prosperity and creature-comforts.
- Burroughs, *Moses his Choice*, self-denial and better treasure.
- Watson, *Godly Man’s Picture*, changed valuation / prizing Christ.
- Baxter, *Crucifying of the World*, love of God and self-denial.
