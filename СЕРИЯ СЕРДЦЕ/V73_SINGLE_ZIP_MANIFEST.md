## Core discovery

V73 closes a major practical gap: how true but weak grace grows toward a more confirmed state without turning sanctification into self-salvation.

```text
Flavel: keeping the heart presupposes regeneration.
Owen: spiritual mindedness is fruit/evidence of life, not ground of peace with God.
Baxter: weak Christian ≠ seeming Christian; weak grace must grow toward confirmed grace.
Scudder: daily walk gives ordinary structure to piety.
Watson: marks of godliness confirm and test, but do not replace Christ.
```

## Best article formula

```text
Dead heart: needs regeneration.
Weak living heart: needs strengthening.
Seeming Christian: needs exposure.
Confirmed Christian: still needs watchfulness.
All: need Christ as root, not marks or duties as substitute root.
```
