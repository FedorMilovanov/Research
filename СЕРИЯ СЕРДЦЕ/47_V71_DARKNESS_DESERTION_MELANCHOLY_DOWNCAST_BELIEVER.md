# V71 — Darkness / Desertion / Melancholy / Downcast Believer

**Дата:** 2026-06-25  
**Режим:** продолжение без распухания архива. Добавлен один новый том и точечные вставки в существующие карты/ledger.  
**Тема:** как отличить мёртвое сердце, ложный мир, временную веру и настоящую, но омрачённую / избитую / меланхоличную благодать.

---

## 1. Зачем нужен V71

После V68–V70 уже есть сильный слой:

- almost Christian;
- false peace;
- common grace vs saving grace;
- temporary faith;
- preparatory convictions;
- afflicted conscience;
- weak grace.

Но оставалась опасная пастырская дырка: можно разоблачать ложную религиозность так резко, что настоящий верующий в темноте услышит: “значит, ты мёртв”. V71 закрывает эту дыру первоисточниками о **детях света, ходящих во тьме**, о **внутренних смятениях святого**, о **меланхолии**, **дезертировании утешения**, **сатанинских устрашающих стрелах**, **слабой уверенности** и **пастырском различении болезни/искушения/греха/обличения**.

Ключевая формула V71:

```text
False peace can look bright but be dead.
True grace can look dark but be alive.
Melancholy can distort spiritual sight.
Desertion can remove comfort without removing union.
Satanic accusation can imitate conviction but drive away from Christ.
Gospel comfort must not flatter sin, but must not call every darkness unbelief.
```

## 2. Thomas Goodwin — A Child of Light Walking in Darkness

**Primary source:** Thomas Goodwin, *A Child of Light Walking in Darkness*, on Isa. 50:10–11.  
**Source access:** Monergism PDF; Digital Puritan text/scan derivatives; IA scan edition.  
**Status:** high-value primary-source layer; for exact quotation in article, verify against scan/edition locator.

### Why this is gold

Goodwin gives one of the cleanest Puritan distinctions in the whole pack:

1. A true child of God may walk in darkness and lack present light/assurance.
2. Darkness here is not living in known sin as a course, and not mere ignorance, but spiritual discomfiture, sorrow, wounded conscience, and desertion.
3. An unregenerate person may walk in “light” of self-kindled sparks — natural righteousness, legal confidence, outward comforts, self-comfort.
4. Therefore “darkness” and “light” are not automatically proof of state.

### Diagnostic value

Goodwin protects against two opposite errors:

| Error | Goodwin correction |
|---|---|
| “If I have no comfort, I cannot be a child of God.” | A child of God may walk in darkness. |
| “If I feel religiously secure, I must be safe.” | A child of darkness may walk in self-kindled light. |
| “All darkness is evidence of reigning sin.” | Some darkness is distress/desertion, not a life of known rebellion. |
| “All peace is gospel peace.” | Some peace is fire/sparks of one’s own kindling. |

### Article-use formulation

> Не всякий свет — свет Христов, и не всякая темнота — духовная смерть. Есть ложный свет самоправедности, и есть настоящая благодать без видимого утешения. Поэтому пастырская диагностика должна спрашивать не только “светло ли человеку”, а “откуда его свет и к кому ведёт его темнота”.

### Use with the four states

Goodwin does not replace Boston’s four-state map, but gives a crucial guardrail inside it:

```text
state of nature may feel secure
state of grace may feel forsaken
state of glory alone removes darkness fully
```

## 3. William Bridge — A Lifting Up for the Downcast

**Primary source:** William Bridge, *A Lifting Up for the Downcast*, thirteen sermons preached at Stepney, A.D. 1648.  
**Source access:** Monergism PDF; Digital Puritan PDF; Banner/secondary reprints for orientation.  
**Status:** excellent pastoral casuistry source.

### Why this is gold

Bridge is not merely “comfort literature.” His table of cases is a diagnostic map:

- great sins;
- weakness of grace;
- miscarriage of duties;
- want of assurance;
- temptation;
- desertion;
- affliction;
- unserviceableness;
- discouragements drawn from the condition itself;
- final cure by faith in Jesus Christ.

This is exactly the pastoral layer needed after V70. V70 says temporary faith can look strong but be rootless. V71 adds: true grace can be downcast for many reasons and must not be misdiagnosed.

### Pastoral value

Bridge teaches that discouragement can arise not only from gross sin, but also from:

- weakness after real conversion;
- failure in duties even where duty is loved;
- lack of assurance;
- temptation that terrifies rather than entices;
- felt desertion;
- afflictions and uselessness.

### Article-use formulation

> Пуритане не лечили всех одним диагнозом. Один человек подавлен из-за скрытого греха, другой — из-за слабой благодати, третий — из-за неудачи в обязанностях, четвёртый — из-за отсутствия уверенности, пятый — из-за искушения, шестой — из-за ощущения оставленности. Одна и та же внешняя подавленность может требовать разных слов закона и Евангелия.

## 4. Richard Sibbes — The Soul’s Conflict With Itself

**Primary source:** Richard Sibbes, *The Soul’s Conflict With Itself*.  
**Source access:** Monergism PDF; Digital Puritan PDF; IA scans.  
**Status:** essential source for sorrow, disquiet, excessive dejection, true comforters, and difference between good men and others in conflicts with sin.

### Why this is gold

Sibbes opens with two visible-church categories:

- those kept by Satan in false peace;
- those whom Satan keeps in bondage and woundedness.

This is a perfect bridge between V69/V70 and V71: the pastor must both disturb false peace and relieve destructive bondage.

The table of contents itself shows why this work belongs in the pack:

- discouragements from without;
- discouragements from within;
- casting down ourselves by sorrow;
- remedies of casting down;
- difference between good men and others in conflicts with sin;
- unfitting/excessive dejection;
- disquiets of soul;
- help by others and true comforters;
- promises and evidences;
- quieting the spirit in troubles for sin.

### Diagnostic value

Sibbes keeps four truths together:

```text
1. false peace is spiritually deadly;
2. real sorrow can become excessive and disordering;
3. conflict with sin differs in good men and others;
4. comfort must be applied with method, not slogans.
```

### Article-use formulation

> Есть души, которых надо вывести из ложного мира, и есть души, которых надо вынести из ложной тьмы. Один и тот же призыв “проверяй сердце” может быть лекарством для самоуверенного и ядом для уже сокрушённого, если не различить состояние.

## 5. Timothy Rogers — Trouble of Mind and the Disease of Melancholy

**Primary source:** Timothy Rogers, *A Discourse Concerning Trouble of Mind, and the Disease of Melancholy*, first published 1691; IA has 1706 second edition with letters from divines.  
**Source access:** Internet Archive 1706 scan; EEBO2/TCP searchable text; Monergism PDF.  
**Status:** crucial for not over-spiritualizing every darkness.

### Why this is gold

Rogers is one of the most important voices for this series because he treats **trouble of mind** and **melancholy** together. This does not make him modern psychology; it makes him a careful pastor who knows that bodily/mental condition, spiritual fear, conscience, assurance, sin, temptation and suffering can be entangled.

### Diagnostic value

Rogers helps build a safer pastoral grid:

| Case | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| faithful follower overcome with doubts/fears | not every doubt means hypocrisy |
| person losing assurance through neglect | real pastoral admonition may be needed |
| person losing peace after grievous sin | do not cheap-comfort sin, but do not deny mercy |
| person crushed by trauma/loss/adversity | suffering may make God appear as enemy in perception |
| melancholic distortion | bodily/mental darkness can distort spiritual conclusions |

### Article-use formulation

> Иногда человек говорит “Бог против меня” не потому, что он богословски пришёл к выводу, а потому что боль, страх, болезнь, потеря, утомление и искушение перекрасили всё внутреннее зрение. Такое состояние нельзя лечить ни голым “перестань унывать”, ни голым “значит, ты невозрождён”.

## 6. Richard Baxter — melancholy, overmuch sorrow, and pastoral/medical realism

**Primary sources:**

- Richard Baxter, *Directions to the Melancholy about their Thoughts* in *A Christian Directory*.
- Richard Baxter, *The Cure of Melancholy and Overmuch Sorrow by Faith and Physick* / later *Preservatives against Melancholy and Overmuch Sorrow*.

**Source access:** EEBO/TCP for *Christian Directory* section; IA 1713 scan for *Preservatives*; Digital Puritan PDF modernized from Practical Works.  
**Status:** use carefully; the older humoral/medical framework should not be imported uncritically, but his pastoral realism is extremely valuable.

### Why this is gold

Baxter is important because he refuses two reductions:

- “It is only spiritual; ignore bodily/medical realities.”
- “It is only bodily; ignore faith, promises, sin, conscience and pastoral counsel.”

The IA 1713 edition itself preserves the old title-formula: cure/preservatives against melancholy and overmuch sorrow by faith and “physick.” That historical wording is not a modern medical protocol, but it proves Baxter did not treat all sorrow as merely a moral failure.

### Article-use formulation

> Пастырская диагностика сердца должна быть богословски твёрдой, но не грубой. Если совесть ранена — лечи совесть. Если грех скрыт — обличай грех. Если вера слаба — укрепляй веру. Если человек меланхолически и телесно истощён — не называй всю тьму “идолом” или “неверием”.

## 7. William Gurnall — terrifying temptations and despair as fiery darts

**Primary source:** William Gurnall, *The Christian in Complete Armour*, especially the shield of faith and Satan’s affrighting temptations.  
**Source access:** CCEL PDF/HTML; IA scans; PRDL listings.  
**Status:** strong source for spiritual warfare, temptation, despair, intrusive blasphemous thoughts and faith’s response.

### Why this is gold

Gurnall distinguishes enticing temptations from **affrighting temptations**: attacks that dismay, terrify, and set the soul on fire with horror. He includes temptations to atheism, blasphemy and despair. This is extremely important for troubled believers because some thoughts horrify the saint precisely because they are hated.

### Diagnostic value

Gurnall gives a critical pastoral distinction:

```text
A thought may be present in the imagination without being welcomed by the will.
Horror at blasphemous thoughts may be evidence of hatred, not consent.
Despair is not humility; it dishonours mercy and closes the window of promise.
Faith answers terror by God, Christ, promise, and the Word.
```

### Article-use formulation

> Не всякая страшная мысль сердца является любимым идолом сердца. Бывают “стрелы”, которые не раскрывают желание души, а мучают её. Вопрос не только “что пришло в ум?”, а “что воля с этим делает — кормит, оправдывает, любит или ненавидит и несёт ко Христу?”

## 8. William Guthrie — storm, adoption, and wanting what God has not yet given

**Primary source:** William Guthrie, *The Christian’s Great Interest*.  
**Source access:** CCEL PDF; IA/other scans.  
**Status:** already introduced earlier; V71 reuses him specifically for darkness/assurance.

### Why this is gold

The prefatory material to Guthrie describes the book as moving the soul from storm under law to calm under the Spirit’s peace; it also says the believer may need to be willing to lack what God is not presently giving. This is a very mature guardrail: assurance is precious, but lack of felt assurance is not identical with lack of interest in Christ.

### Article-use formulation

> Уверенность — дар Божий, а не кнопка, которую человек нажимает по требованию. Пастырь должен вести душу к Христу и к законным основаниям уверенности, но не требовать немедленного одинакового чувства от всех детей Божьих.

## 10. Guardrails for the article series

### Guardrail A — do not make comfort the proof of life

A living believer may lack felt comfort. State is not measured by emotional brightness alone.

### Guardrail B — do not make misery the proof of grace

A troubled conscience may still be in nature if it refuses Christ and rests in self, fear, or reform. Misery is not regeneration.

### Guardrail C — distinguish conviction and accusation

Conviction names sin and drives to Christ. Accusation may use truth-like fragments to drive away from Christ.

### Guardrail D — distinguish intrusive horror from chosen affection

Some thoughts are hated invaders, not cherished idols. The will’s relation to them matters.

### Guardrail E — remember body and temperament without losing theology

Melancholy, exhaustion, trauma, illness and weakness can distort spiritual perception. They do not remove responsibility, but they require gentleness and careful application.

### Guardrail F — state of grace is not state of glory

The believer may be in grace and still experience darkness, temptation, remaining sin, fear, and bodily weakness. Complete undisturbed light belongs to glory.

## 11. Ready-to-use paragraph for the article

Пуританская диагностика сердца была тоньше, чем простая схема: “спокоен — значит здоров” или “тревожен — значит невозрождён”. Goodwin мог говорить о ребёнке света, который ходит во тьме, и о человеке тьмы, который греется у собственных искр. Bridge разбирал подавленного верующего не одним диагнозом, а по разным случаям: великие грехи, слабость благодати, неудачи в обязанностях, отсутствие уверенности, искушение, оставленность, скорбь. Sibbes умел отличать ложный мир от раненого духа. Rogers и Baxter напоминали, что меланхолия и чрезмерная скорбь могут искажать духовное зрение. Gurnall учил, что сатанинская “стрела” может пугать святого именно потому, что она ненавистна ему. Поэтому зрелый пастырский вопрос звучит не так: “тебе светло или темно?”, а так: “откуда твой свет, куда ведёт твоя темнота, на чём стоит твой мир, что делает твоя воля с грехом, и идёшь ли ты от себя ко Христу?”
