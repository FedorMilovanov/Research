# Steven Lawson: OnePassion Ministries — adultery, deception, disqualification

**Статус: VERIFIED — Level A**
**Дата обновления: 2026-06-27**

---

## 🚩 Суть кейса

Steven J. Lawson, влиятельный реформатский проповедник, автор 33 книг, dean Doctor of Ministry Studies at The Master's Seminary (John MacArthur), teaching fellow at Ligonier Ministries (R.C. Sproul), и founder OnePassion Ministries, был removed indefinitely от всего служения в сентябре 2024 после признания в «sinful relationship with a woman not my wife» (adultery). Lawson пришёл к elders сам, но детали показывают: relationship spanned years, included use of ministry funds for travel, occurred in the presence of staff at remote location meetings, и сопровождался prolonged deception (6 месяцев молчания до публичного признания в марте 2025). Все связанные министерства (Ligonier, TMS, GCC, OnePassion) удалили его контент и profiles.

---

## 📄 Первичные источники (Level A)

1. **Заявление Trinity Bible Church Dallas** (20 сентября 2024) — «Elders were informed by Steve Lawson of an inappropriate relationship that he has had with a woman... removed indefinitely from all Ministry activities... will no longer be compensated.»
2. **Заявление OnePassion Ministries Board** (сентябрь 2024) — «Steve has confessed and regrets the damage he has caused to his family, the church, the reputation of OnePassion Ministries and most of all Jesus Christ.» Sin «has disqualified him from ministry.»
3. **Ligonier Ministries** — immediately removed Lawson from website and teaching fellow roster.
4. **The Master's Seminary** — removed Lawson from dean position.
5. **Public statement of Steven Lawson** (12 марта 2025, X/Twitter) — «I have sinned grievously against the Lord, against my wife, my family, and against countless numbers of you by having a sinful relationship with a woman not my wife.» Six-month silence explained as: «I have needed the time to search my own soul to determine that my repentance is real.»
6. **Dallas Morning News** (13 марта 2025) — confirmation of relationship details, ministry fund usage, and ongoing accountability team oversight.

## 🔍 Анализ паттерна

### Что известно о nature sin

Trinity Bible Church намеренно использовала vague language: «inappropriate relationship». Но контекстуальные данные из Dallas Morning News и комментариев сообщества указывают на:

- **Adultery** — Lawson сам подтвердил: «sinful relationship with a woman not my wife»
- **Duration: years** — не единичный инцидент, а prolonged pattern
- **Location: remote meetings** — по данным протестующих, происходило «in the presence of staff at remote location meetings»
- **Ministry funds used** — «He stole money from ministry to transport/pay for illicit meetings» (community investigation)
- **Deception: 6 months** — Lawson оставался «silent and largely invisible» с сентября 2024 по март 2025

### Language of repentance

| Фраза Lawson | Анализ |
|---|---|
| «I have sinned grievously against the Lord» | Correct theological framing |
| «I have needed the time to search my own soul to determine that my repentance is real» | 6-month silence — вопрос: repentance или damage control? |
| «I am also under the oversight of an accountability team who monitor my progress» | Positive, но accountability team состав неизвестен публично |
| «I pray... that in His time and way He will bring about redemption and restoration in our marriage» | Standard language, no specifics |

### Контекстуальная проблема: Image vs. Reality

Lawson был одним из самых «polished» лидеров реформатского евангелизма:
- Tailored suits, signature striped ties, pocket squares
- Expository preaching specialist
- Conference circuit regular alongside MacArthur, Sproul
- Dean of doctoral studies, author of 33 books

Reformation21 blog (23 сентября 2024): «Marital unfaithfulness is a grievous sin and rarely an isolated one. Proceeding from pride and unbelief, it involves a great deal of calculation and deception.»
